About
This portfolio demonstrates applied research skills across financial econometrics, sustainability analytics, financial analytics, and sustainable finance. Each project follows a complete research workflow: formulating a research question, collecting and processing data, applying rigorous methodology, and interpreting results. Projects use real data from Yahoo Finance, SEC EDGAR, World Bank API, and CPI/OECD where available, with simulated data calibrated to published distributions for the remainder.

A Note on Methodology

This portfolio uses an AI-assisted research workflow. All research questions, methodological choices, and result interpretations are directed by the researcher. Code implementation is assisted by Claude AI — reflecting a modern, collaborative approach to data-intensive research. Using AI as a coding collaborator is an increasingly valued skill in finance and academia, enabling researchers to focus on the analytical thinking that matters most while maintaining full transparency about the workflow.
